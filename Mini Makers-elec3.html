Analogue and Digital

Pins

Each of the pins can be set to INPUT or OUTPUT

INPUT

OUTPUT



Analogue

  • Anything that has more than two values
  • It has numbers
  • It has a needle
  • It's continuous - you can get in-between values
  • Found in nature and environment i.e. time, distance, height, temperature, humidity, etc.


Digital

  • Only TWO values: ON or OFF
  • It's also described as HIGH or LOW
  • Often controlled by a switch
  • Not found in nature - we derive it from some reference.
  • e.g. If signal is > 3V, it's HIGH. 0V is LOW.


Analogue Signal Graph

  • Smooth waves
  • Continuous


Digital Signal Graph

  • Square waves
  • Only two values
